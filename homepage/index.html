About

I am a PhD candidate in the database group of UChicago. My advisor is Prof. Aaron Elmore. I also work with Prof. Sanjay Krishnan and Prof. Michael Franklin.

Research Interest

My research interest includes designing resource-efficient query processing methods on dynamic datasets, providing query service in the cloud, and high-performance transaction processing on multicore machines. I am currently working on CrocodileDB, a new database architecture that exploits time slackness to enable new resource-efficient query execution plans. Please check out our vision paper for more information.
